Person One

This person is a young women the age of 22. She loves the outdoors and animals. She loves hiking, boating, walking with friends. She has a dog that loves to go with her too. This person shops for her own food, cooks healthly meals, and exercised regularly. Her favorite colors are blue and yellow. She listens to fun upbeat music. When she is not able to get outside, she likes to read and write in her journal.

Person two

This person is an older man about 55. He is active in his church and has many friends. He is about to retire and wants to learn how to do more things outside. He and his wife have just bought a motorcycle and plan on taking many trips around the country. He is a outdoors person but likes to travel and stay in hotels. Sleeping in the outdoors is no longer something he wants to do. He has family and they live in different areas of the country. He spends time indoors working on projects when the weather is bad.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be like today?
  • Can I take a hike this afternoon?
  • What is causing the clouds to form like this?
  • Where can I find the time for the 5K?
  • How can I support the youth?
